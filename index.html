About AniRank

Who we are and why we built this.

🎯

Our Mission

AniRank exists to give real, honest anime reviews without the hype. No paid promotions. No sponsored rankings. Just a fan who watches everything and tells you straight up what's worth your time.

🚫

No Spoilers — Ever

Every review on this site is written to help you decide whether to watch something — without ruining the experience. We describe the vibe, not the plot twists. You're safe here.

⭐

How We Score

Scores are out of 10. We judge on story, animation, characters, pacing, and emotional impact. A 9.0+ means essential viewing. An 8.0+ is very good. Anything below 7.5 doesn't make it onto the site.

💰

How This Site Makes Money

This site earns a small commission if you sign up for Crunchyroll through our links. It costs you nothing extra and helps us keep reviewing anime. We only recommend services we actually use and trust.
